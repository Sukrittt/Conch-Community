WHY DID WE START?

              When I was talking to my mother about purchasing a ticket one
              day, the first thing I saw when I opened my email was an
              advertisement for an airline. It was shocking. You may have had
              similar experiences, which served as the inspiration for Conch and
              for those who do not regard themselves as Big Tech products ready
              to be sold to advertisers.
WHAT DO WE DO?

            We offer a platform for the community where users can access
            private networks to protect their privacy and take control of their
            data. Our platform gives users the tools they need to do this.
HOW DID WE DO IT?

              The activity you do on the platform like using mail, social media
              etc. and the data it contains will be encrypted & then stored on
              the blockchain or you can store it on your system which only you
              can access with a key. You are the owner of your precious data
              with full autonomy.
HOW DOES IT WORK?

              When you join the community, you are granted access to all of the 
              tools, services, events, portals, and other resources available to 
              members.
